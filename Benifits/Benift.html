What is Chit?

“Chit” means a transaction ( whether called
chit fund, chit, kuri or by any other name),
by which the foreman enters into an
agreement with a number of subscribers that
everyone of them shall subscribe a certain
sum for a certain period and each subscriber
in his turn as determined by lot or by auction,
shall be entitled to a prized amount.
